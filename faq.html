Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and facilities

What are your pickup and drop-off times?

+

Our standard pickup and drop-off hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday - Sunday: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM

We offer early drop-off (6:30 AM) and late pickup (8:30 PM) for an additional fee of $5 per service. Please let us know in advance if you need these extended hours.

Do I need to book in advance?

+

Yes, advance booking is highly recommended, especially for:

  • Holiday periods and school vacations
  • Weekend care
  • Long-term stays (one week or more)

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for peak times. Same-day bookings may be available depending on availability, but please call ahead to check.

Can I visit the facility before booking?

+

Absolutely! We encourage prospective clients to visit our facility. Tours are available:

  • Monday - Friday: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

Please call ahead to schedule a tour so we can ensure someone is available to show you around and answer your questions personally.

Can I bring my dog's own food?

+

Yes, we encourage you to bring your dog's regular food to maintain their diet and avoid digestive issues. Please:

  • Label food containers with your dog's name
  • Provide feeding instructions
  • Bring enough for the entire stay plus extra
  • Inform us of any dietary restrictions or allergies

If you prefer, we can provide high-quality premium food for an additional $5 per day.

How often will my dog be walked?

+

Walking frequency depends on your chosen package:

  • Day Package: 2 walks daily
  • Classic Package: 3 walks daily
  • Premium Package: 4 walks daily

All walks include forest trail exploration, playtime, and bathroom breaks. Additional walks can be arranged for $8 per walk.

Will my dog be with other dogs?

+

Yes, socialization is part of our care program, but we carefully manage interactions:

  • Dogs are grouped by size, temperament, and energy level
  • All dogs undergo temperament assessment
  • Staff supervision during all group activities
  • Private time available for dogs who prefer solitude

We prioritize your dog's comfort and safety above all else.

What vaccinations are required?

+

All dogs must have current vaccinations:

  • Rabies: Required by law
  • DHPP: Distemper, Hepatitis, Parainfluenza, Parvovirus
  • Bordetella: Kennel cough (required every 6 months)

Please provide vaccination records at least 48 hours before your dog's first visit. Puppies must have completed their primary vaccination series.

What happens if my dog gets sick during their stay?

+

Your dog's health is our top priority. If illness occurs:

  • 24/7 on-call veterinary services available
  • We'll contact you immediately
  • Minor issues handled by our trained staff
  • Emergency transport to partner vet clinic if needed

All medical decisions will be made in consultation with you, and we'll keep you updated throughout any treatment.

How do you handle emergencies?

+

We have comprehensive emergency protocols:

  • First aid-trained staff on-site at all times
  • Partner veterinary clinics within 10 minutes
  • Emergency transport arrangements
  • Detailed emergency contact procedures

We conduct regular emergency drills and maintain detailed medical records for all guests.

What are the sleeping arrangements?

+

We offer comfortable sleeping options:

  • Individual climate-controlled rooms
  • Orthopedic bedding provided
  • Personal items from home welcome
  • Nightlight and calming music available

Rooms are cleaned daily and maintained at optimal temperature and humidity levels.

Is the facility secure?

+

Security is our highest priority:

  • 24/7 video surveillance
  • Double-gated entry systems
  • 6-foot high fencing with dig barriers
  • Fire and security alarm systems
  • Staff on premises 24/7

We conduct regular security checks and maintain all safety equipment to the highest standards.

What if my dog has special needs?

+

We accommodate many special needs:

  • Medication administration
  • Special dietary requirements
  • Mobility assistance
  • Behavioral modification support
  • Senior dog care protocols

Please discuss your dog's specific needs during booking so we can provide the best possible care.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Feel free to contact us with any additional questions you may have.
